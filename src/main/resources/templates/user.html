Why choose us?

Fresh, modern and with that special something - that's what makes our floristry special. Large bouquets, small gifts, arrangements for celebrations, companies or at home - we have something for every taste. Be inspired by our color and style corners and be advised by our competent team.

Also in our range you will find a diverse selection of Bingenheim seeds in proven Demeter quality for your own vegetable and herb cultivation, because sustainability and the preservation of a healthy environment are very important to us. Therefore, whenever possible, we obtain our goods and flowers from local growers in order to reduce unnecessarily long transport routes as far as possible.
